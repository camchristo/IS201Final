Why I Train

The gym is one of my favorite ways to stay disciplined, improve my energy, and build a stronger base for sports performance. I like training because it gives me a challenge, keeps me sharp, and helps me see what consistency can do over time.

This page is a fun look at what I enjoy about lifting, what I focus on in my workouts, and why fitness matters to me.

My Workout Priorities

  1. Build strength
    • Bench press
    • Leg Press
    • Seated Cable Row
  2. Improve athletic performance
    • Explosive leg work
    • Sled pushes
    • Sprint-focused conditioning
  3. Stay consistent
    • Train even on busy weeks
    • Keep workouts simple and effective
    • Focus on long-term progress

Gym Mindset

I like workouts that feel competitive, challenging, and useful beyond the gym itself. Plus I get to make more friends!
